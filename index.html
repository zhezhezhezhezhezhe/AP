How To Play

Defend The Core

1. Build Click glowing build nodes beside the road, then choose a tower from the circular menu.
2. Upgrade Click a tower to inspect it, upgrade it, or sell it for coins.
3. Survive Press Start Wave. Enemies follow multiple roads and merge at the CORE.
4. Adapt Use Frost for control, Blast for groups, Laser for armor, and Pulse for steady damage.

Tower Guide

PulseCheap and reliable. Good early defense for single targets.
LaserHigh damage and partial armor piercing. Best against Shield and Boss enemies.
FrostSlows enemies so other towers get more shots. Strong near shared route coverage.
BlastArea damage. Best against Swarm groups and crowded lanes.
Watch for Shield, Regen, Phantom, and Boss enemies. If Core Health reaches zero, the run ends.
